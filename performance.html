World Drum Night VI: Songs Of Our Land

It was an honor that we were able to perform at the sixth installment of World Drum Night, our biggest event of the year. World drum night took place at Republic Polytechnics Culture Center on the 2nd of November 2024. This event brought together drum groups from all over Singapore, from Zingo festival drums, POLITE groups like us and even some alumni groups, includng some of our alumni in the group Hatsu Ryo. We performed a piece that our members wrote by the name "Kinou no saki", a song conveying our togetherness and unity, as well as the legacy that we will leave for future generations of NP Taiko. Kinou no saki was also mixed with Haruka, written by Kodo, Reach out for the skies by Rui En and Taufik Batisah and Our Singapore by JJ Lin
