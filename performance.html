SP Arts Fiesta 2024: Japanese Matsuri

We were provided with the opportunity to perform the opening piece for Singapore Polytechnic's Arts Fiesta 2024: Japanese Matsuri on the 28th of October. The event kicked off Singapore Polytechnic's art series and involved all 3 Japanese cultural clubs from Singapore, Nanyang and Ngee Ann Polytechnics. We brought the high electrifying energy with our performances of 2 songs, Tomodachi and Isami-goma, potraying happiness, community and friendship.

World Drum Night VI: Songs Of Our Land

It was an honor that we were able to perform at the sixth installment of World Drum Night, our biggest event of the year. World drum night took place at Republic Polytechnics Culture Center on the 2nd of November 2024. This event brought together drum groups from all over Singapore, from Zingo festival drums, POLITE groups like us and even some alumni groups, includng some of our alumni in the group Hatsu Ryo. We performed a piece that our members wrote by the name "Kinou no saki", a song conveying our togetherness and unity, as well as the legacy that we will leave for future generations of NP Taiko.
